🎮 Player Controls

Mouse Controls:

  • • Click play/pause button or video to toggle playback
  • • Click and drag progress bar to seek
  • • Click volume button to mute/unmute
  • • Drag volume slider to adjust volume
  • • Click speed button to change playback rate
  • • Click fullscreen button to toggle fullscreen

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • • Space - Play/Pause
  • • M - Mute/Unmute
  • • F - Toggle Fullscreen
  • • ←/→ - Seek 10 seconds
  • • ↑/↓ - Volume up/down
